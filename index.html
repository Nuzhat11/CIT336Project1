About Me

My name is Nuzhat Chetona Bhuiyan. I am originally from Bangladesh. I have lived there until I was 12. I lived in Texas for a year, from there, I moved to New Jersey. I lived in New Jersey for 5 years. I got accepted to Pace University after graduating from high school, and I moved to New York for college.

I am an only child. When I tell people that, I often get asked if I ever felt alone. I didnt: Mainly because growing up, I liked playing, drawing by myself. Also because never knew how it would be to have a sibling.

In addition to English, I speak Bangla and Hindi fluently.

In Pace University, my major is MIS, and my minor is Business Management. I have dormed in Pace University for my first two years, and had the most incredible time living the city life. Due to some unfortunate events, I had to take a year and a half off. During which, I moved to Queens. After my time off, now I am finally back to Pace trying to complete my undergrad.
